Mike McKean

Illustrator, Animator, Designer

I'm an entertainment-based artist, with a love for characters and animation. My goal is to be intertwined in the field of cartoon animation, as I tend to focus on storyboarding, character design, and animation.

While spending a year and a half studying engineering after high school, it became very apparent to me that my artistic aspirations were quickly overpowering my desire to be an engineer. I had no choice but to drop out and begin my studies at College for Creative Studies. However, that period of my education left me with a variety of technical skills, and later even helped me create my own website (you're looking at it!). I see myself as an intelligent, dedicated and inspired individual, and I can't wait to see what new projects are in store for me.
